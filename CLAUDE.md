Totem, trap, and seal bar addon for WoW Classic. Provides class-specific action bars with cooldown timers and keybinding support for Shamans, Hunters, and Paladins. Originally created by Floraline, ported to Classic by paradosi.

## Project-Specific Context

- Supports three classes: Shaman (4 totem bars), Hunter (trap bar), Paladin (seal bar)
- No AI attribution anywhere
